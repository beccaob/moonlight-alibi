Who Are We?

Fronted by the charismatic Jamie, Moonlight Alibi electrifies the stage with their infectious energy and magnetic stage presence.
Their performances are a non-stop celebration of rock 'n' roll's greatest hits, guaranteed to get you and your guests dancing and singing along to every song.
The band members' virtuosity on their instruments is evident in their seamless renditions of classic tunes. Ryan delivers scorching guitar solos, Pa holds down the rhythm like no other with thunderous basslines, Becca adds a touch of vintage charm with her keys, and Chris keeps the beat pumping with precision and flair.

Why Choose Us?

Picture this: a fusion of timeless classics and fiery rock anthems, all carefully curated to set your special day ablaze.
