About Us

Formed in 2016, Moonlight Alibi are a group of passionate musicians who share a deep love for the music that shaped generations. With a diverse range of influences spanning from Elvis Presley and Chuck Berry to The Rolling Stones and The Beatles, Moonlight Alibi embodies the true spirit of rock and roll. Their performances are a non-stop celebration of rock 'n' roll's greatest hits, guaranteed to get you and your guests dancing and singing along to every song.

Fronted by the charismatic Jamie, he electrifies the stage with his infectious energy and magnetic stage presence.
The band members' virtuosity on their instruments is evident in their seamless renditions of classic tunes. Ryan delivers scorching guitar solos, Pa holds down the rhythm like no other with thunderous basslines, Becca adds a touch of vintage charm with her keys, and Chris keeps the beat pumping with precision and flair.
